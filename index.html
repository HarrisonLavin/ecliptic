:DRONE- Choose an opponent and guess a non-:DRONE card. If that opponent has that card, they are out of the round. Has no effect if they are protected by a :REPLICANT.
:FIELD_TECH- Choose an opponent. You may look at their hand, if they are not protected by a :REPLICANT.
:STRIKER- Compare hands with another player. Whoever has the lower value card is out of the round. Cannot be played against a player protected by a :REPLICANT.
:REPLICANT- Until the start of your next turn, you ignore the effects of any card played against you.
:CHAIRMAN- Choose a player, including yourself. That player discards their hand, and draws a new one. If all other players are protected by a :REPLICANT, you must play this card on yourself and abide by its effects.
:CMNDR- Exchange hands with another player. Has no effect on a player protected by a :REPLICANT.
:OPERATIVE- Must be discarded if you have a :CMNDR or :CHAIRMAN in hand. May be discarded at any other time to no effect otherwise.
:ECLIPTECH- If you discard this card, you are out of the round.

:ECLIPTIC is an adaptation of Alderac Entertainment Group's card game Love Letter, programmed entirely in JavaScript. Plugins and libraries used include jQuery and jQuery UI.

To play, drag a card from your hand into the center of the playfield. The game will recognize the value and effects of the card, and enact them on your A.I. opponent. Should either player be eliminated before the end of the round, their opponent scores one point.
If, at the end of the round, both players are still present, the player with the higher value card wins the round. First player to 7 points is the winner.
